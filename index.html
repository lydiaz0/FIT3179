Australian Domestic Airline Analysis (2019 vs 2020)

Due to Covid, people's lifestyle has been changed thoroughtly.
The following provides an analysis on how daily travel been affected.

How do domestic passengers being influenced?

In order to control the spread of Covid-19, non-essential travel is not encourage across Australia.
The amount of passengers reduced significantly in 2020.

Domestic passengers vs. International passengers

All international travel is banned, unless necessary for business, aid assistance or compassionate reasons, or passengers are ordinarily residents in a country other than Australia.
Similar with domestic passengers, the amount of international passengers arrive/depart from Australia also reduced.

How do passengers' travel preferences change?

As a result of travel ban and lockdowns, passengers' preference on travelly also vary.
